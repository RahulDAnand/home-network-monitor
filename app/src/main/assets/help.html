There are typically two cases to exclude a device from your wireless network.

 

Case 1 - Deny device from clients list.

Step-1: Enter the login credentials.

Step-2: Find the wireless clients list and Disable the device.

Case 2 - Configure the MAC address filter to block the device.

Step-1: Enter the login credentials.

Step-2: Locate the device you want to block. Copy its MAC address.

Step-3: Find the MAC address filter. Enter the device MAC address on the blacklist and Apply the settings.
